Subject Lines:

Subject Lines aren’t sentences, so don’t use period(.)

Greeting:

X: Dear Client,
X: Dear both,

X: Dear Boss Huang,
X: Dears,

The Body:

These are usually separated by paragraphs
Don’t overusing exclamation mark(!)

Closings:

X: Best Regards, (“regards” is correct)

[Mac] 設定開啟檔案的預設軟體

近期在mac的環境上有點卡關，像是環境的PATH曾被我改壞… 還好朋友有幫我救回來= =…
